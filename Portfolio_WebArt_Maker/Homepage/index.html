Biography

I’m Mariana Perfeito, and I come from the charming city of Porto, Portugal. My journey is a unique one, as I transitioned from a background in architecture to a thriving career as a UI/UX developer.

Originally, I attended the Faculty of Architecture of Porto. It was there that I honed my skills in spatial design and aesthetics. However, my insatiable curiosity and a profound fascination with the digital world led me down a different path.

My Way

My design vision is both different and unique. I continually challenge conventions, explore fresh perspectives, and craft experiences that stand out through innovation and originality. Every project represents an opportunity to turn creative ideas into reality.

About my work

Website Design

Website design harmonizes aesthetics and functionality for an engaging online experience. It encompasses layout, color, and navigation, ensuring a visually appealing and intuitive interface. Responsive design is key for compatibility across devices. Success lies in effective communication of a brand's message through user-friendly design.

Brand Design

Brand design is the process of crafting a visual identity that reflects a company's values and personality. It encompasses the creation of a unique logo, color palette, typography, and other visual elements that distinguish the brand. Effective brand design not only establishes recognition but also communicates the brand's ethos to the target.

UI/UX Design

UI/UX design focuses on enhancing user satisfaction and interaction with digital products. User Interface (UI) involves crafting visually appealing and intuitive interfaces, encompassing elements like layout, colors, and typography. User Experience (UX) delves into the overall user journey, ensuring seamless navigation.

Website Development

Web developers use various programming languages, frameworks, and tools to build responsive, dynamic, and secure websites. They often work closely with web designers to implement the visual aspects and ensure a seamless user experience. As technology evolves, developers adapt to new trends, ensuring websites are functional.

Visual Design

Effective visual design not only captures attention but also communicates the intended message or brand identity. Designers aim to create visually appealing and harmonious compositions that resonate with the target audience. Attention to detail, consistency, and a deep understanding of design principles contribute to the success.
